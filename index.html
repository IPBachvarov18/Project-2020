Our project

Our project is a boat. That is a vehicle, which isn't pollute the oceans, seas and the air. Powering the boat with fitness exercises is our idea. When somebody is training the boat is charging generators, which causes set off of the boat. If there is some machine to create so much power the boat will be so fast. Our plan is to keep the oceans and seas clean, because they are our main source of water. And the air because we breathe it.
